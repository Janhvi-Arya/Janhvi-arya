## 🌸 Hey there, I'm Janhvi!
<img align="right" width="340" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/L1R1tvI9svkIWwpVYr/giphy.gif" alt="girl coding gif"/>

> *"From wiring networks to architecting clouds — every skill I've built has led me here."*

I'm an **Electronics & Telecommunication Engineer** (B.E., 78.12% 🎓) who dove headfirst into the **Enterprise Networking world** at **Wysetek Systems Technologists Pvt. Ltd.** — one of Mumbai's leading IT infrastructure companies.

For over a year, I've been in the trenches:
- 🔧 Configuring **Cisco, Meraki & SG series** enterprise switches
- 🩺 Diagnosing and resolving **L2/L3 network failures** under pressure
- 🛡️ Designing **VLAN segmentation**, trunking, and STP topologies
- 📡 Keeping critical network infrastructure running **24/7**

This deep-rooted networking expertise gave me an unfair advantage — I understand **how infrastructure actually works** at the packet level, which makes me a stronger cloud engineer than most.

Now, I'm channeling everything into **Cloud & DevOps Engineering** — because the future is serverless, containerized, and automated. And I plan to build it. 🚀
